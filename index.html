Beauty: Always Appealing to the Eyes

Short Poem: Eyes of the Universe

Her eyes, a gateway to perfection's grace,
A love that holds you, never to misplace.
That gaze which captures for a fleeting moment,
A universe's wonder, a cosmic ornament.

Short Poem 2: Earth's Angel

If beauty sought a form to represent,
You'd be the choice, its essence to present.
Proud is he who calls himself your man,
An earth's angel, a rare and wondrous plan.

Haiku: Tears of the Divine

Rain: Tears of a God
Mourns a life that's lost, yet gives
Life anew, a gift.
